# Demo Opportunity

Title: Workforce Analytics and Reporting Modernization Support
Buyer: California Employment Development Department
Estimated Value: $320,000
Deadline: 2026-04-10
Delivery Model: Remote-first with quarterly onsite workshops in Sacramento

## Summary

The agency seeks a vendor to modernize workforce reporting workflows, improve data quality, and deliver executive dashboards for labor program performance. The project includes AWS-based data engineering, dashboard design, and automation of recurring reporting.

## Mandatory Requirements

- demonstrated public-sector data modernization experience
- experience with AWS-based data pipelines
- ability to build executive dashboards
- support for quarterly onsite workshops in California

## Preferred Requirements

- labor or workforce domain experience
- accessibility-aware dashboard design
- workflow automation experience

## Risks

- procurement team expects a concise implementation approach
- vendor should show similar prior work with measurable reporting outcomes
