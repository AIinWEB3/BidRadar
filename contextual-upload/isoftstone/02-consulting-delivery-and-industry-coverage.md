# iSoftStone Consulting Delivery and Industry Coverage

## Consulting Positioning

Public materials describe iSoftStone Consulting as focused on digital transformation across multiple industries, with emphasis on combining strategy, data, and implementation.

## Industries Explicitly Mentioned

- manufacturing
- transportation
- energy
- high-tech
- retail
- logistics

## Delivery and Consulting Strength Indicators

- iSoftStone Consulting states that it relies on eight major delivery bases nationwide for localized implementation support.
- Public materials describe a digital talent pool with strategic and data consulting capability.
- A public consulting page states that more than 72% of consultants have over 10 years of consulting experience.
- iSoftStone Consulting describes an end-to-end capability from strategy to IT.
- Public materials reference a consulting delivery model called "Four Controls, Seven Managements."

## What This Suggests for Opportunity Fit

Good fit indicators:

- digital transformation consulting
- data consulting and implementation
- industry modernization programs
- projects needing strategy-to-execution support

Weaker fit indicators:

- opportunities that depend heavily on public proof of U.S. federal prime-contract past performance
- opportunities requiring highly specialized defense or classified delivery experience

## Retrieval-Friendly Summary

iSoftStone's public consulting materials suggest a firm with meaningful transformation consulting capacity, strong delivery scale, and cross-industry experience, especially where strategy, data, and implementation need to be combined.
