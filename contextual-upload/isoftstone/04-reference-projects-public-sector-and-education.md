# iSoftStone Reference Projects: Public-Sector and Education Adjacent Examples

## Purpose

This file captures public examples that may be relevant when testing opportunity fit for transportation, education, and government-adjacent work.

## Example 1: Hangzhou Public Transport Group Office Computer Procurement

Public materials describe a project in which Softcom Computing, part of the broader iSoftStone ecosystem, supplied office computers and related technical support to Hangzhou Public Transport Group.

Publicly described outcomes include:

- improved informatization support for transport operations
- more stable dispatching support
- better operational efficiency and office productivity

Qualification interpretation:

- this is evidence of transportation-sector and public-service-adjacent delivery
- it is more infrastructure and equipment oriented than consulting-led transformation

## Example 2: Chengdu University of Information Technology Project

Public materials describe a project where Softcom Computing provided computer equipment and technical support to Chengdu University of Information Technology.

Publicly described outcomes include:

- improved teaching and research environment
- support for professional software and scientific research computing
- better data processing and research productivity

Qualification interpretation:

- this is evidence of education-sector delivery and IT implementation support
- it is not the same as enterprise consulting or major software modernization by itself

## How to Use These Examples in Bid Qualification

Potential strengths:

- transportation or education sector familiarity
- public-institution adjacency
- IT implementation and enablement experience

Potential gaps:

- these examples do not independently prove U.S. federal contracting history
- these examples do not independently prove complex procurement-program management
- these examples are more relevant to sector familiarity than to deep proposal compliance evidence
